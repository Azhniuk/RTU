TARAKANIV FORT

Aristocratic Dubno fortress resembles British colonial architecture with its exquisite lines, the same flow of the outline, a great number of arcades and architectural decoration.
An excellent landmark of the fortification art of the 19th century located in the suburbs of Tarakaniv village, Rivne oblast, is abandoned today, and thus is the most mystic destination in Ukraine.
Don't miss the opportunity to get inside through a 100-meter long tunnel!
Learn more here

In the 19th century, it was built by imperial government to protect the Kyiv - Lviv railway.
The construction has a rhombus shape with sides of about 240 meters. Outer walls of the fort are now almost indistinguishable from the outside. But if you get to the inner area, everything looks pretty good.
Today the fortress, being taken over by time and physical factors, fell into disrepute. People wander around by day; at night the sounds of a ghostly combat march is heard...
